Welcome! I am a Master's student in Astronomy at the **University of Cape Town**, specialising in **X-ray astronomy** and **Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN)**.

My current research focuses on the X-ray spectral analysis and full-spectrum spectral energy distribution (SED) fitting of local galaxies from the 12-Micron Galaxy Sample (12MGS). I work with data from X-ray observatories such as **Chandra** and **NuSTAR**, as well as radio data from **MeerKAT**, to characterise the properties of AGN and their host galaxies.
