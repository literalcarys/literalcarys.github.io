## First-Author

**A New Hope for AGN SED Fitting: X-Ray Spectral Analysis of 12MGS AGN with the C2PO-Torus Model**
*C.J.E. Gilbert et al.*
In prep, undergoing internal review. Expected submission to MNRAS in early 2026.

This paper presents the X-ray spectral analysis of 43 AGN from the 12-Micron Galaxy Sample of IR-bright galaxies, along with a new physically-based X-ray spectral model, C2PO-Torus, designed to assist with SED fitting.
